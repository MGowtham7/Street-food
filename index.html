International Street Food Recipes

International street food is a vibrant reflection of diverse culinary traditions, with standout dishes like Tacos from Mexico, which consist of corn tortillas filled with grilled meats, onions, cilantro, and salsa. Pani Puri from India offers a delightful combination of crispy puris filled with spiced chickpeas and potatoes, served with flavored water. In Vietnam, the Banh Mi features a baguette stuffed with grilled pork or chicken, pickled vegetables, and spicy mayonnaise, while Arepas from Venezuela and Colombia are versatile cornmeal patties that can be grilled or fried and filled with cheese, meats, or avocados.
